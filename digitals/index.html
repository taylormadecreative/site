SC.05 — FAQ
What exactly are digitals?

Clean, natural, straight-to-camera photos on a simple background — minimal styling, no heavy retouching. They show agencies and casting exactly what you look like right now, which is why they're the standard for agency submissions, comp cards, and casting profiles.

How much do digitals cost, and how long is the session?

$100 flat for a 30-minute session, paid online when you book. No quote, no back-and-forth — pick a time and it's locked.

When can I book?

Any day of the week, 9am to 9pm Central. The calendar shows real openings from my live production schedule, and your slot is confirmed the moment payment clears.

What should I wear?

Bring 2–3 simple outfit options — solid colors photograph best. Arrive with hair done and light, natural makeup. A prep email with the full checklist hits your inbox before shoot day.

Where do we shoot?

At my studio in Downtown Dallas, or on location — leave a location preference when you book and we'll sort it before shoot day.

How do I get my photos?

Through your own private client portal. Your frames from the session are posted there for you to view, proof, and pick from.

Can I reschedule?

Life happens. Reach out and we'll figure it out together — reply to any email from me or send a message through the client portal, and we'll land on a date that works.
