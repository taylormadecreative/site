What platforms do you create content for?

Wherever your audience is. Short-form video and designed posts are built to travel — the same reel or carousel works across platforms, so the engine feeds every channel you run instead of locking you into one.

Who owns the content?

You do. Everything I shoot, design, and edit for your brand is delivered through your private client portal, and it's yours to post, repurpose, and keep.

Do I have to be on camera?

No. Product shots, b-roll, designed carousels, and AI-assisted formats all carry a feed without a face on screen. If you want to be on camera, I'll direct you; if you don't, the content still works.

What does monthly content cost?

Retainers are quoted per project, because cadence, formats, and shoot needs drive the number. Send a short inquiry through the booking page and I reply within one business day with a real quote.

How do we start?

Start with an inquiry on the booking page — tell me about your business and what your feed needs to do. You get a reply within one business day with a recommended plan and a quote.
