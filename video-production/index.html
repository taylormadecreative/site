How much does video production cost in DFW?

Every production is quoted per project, because scope drives cost — concept, locations, crew needs, and edit complexity. Send an inquiry with your idea and budget range and I reply within one business day with a real number. A 50% deposit locks your production date.

What do you shoot on?

Motion runs on the Sony FX6 cinema camera; stills on the Sony A7RV. Direction, production, and edit all happen in-house.

How long until I get my video?

About two weeks after the shoot, with two rounds of edits included. Need it faster? Rush timelines get scoped in your quote.

Do music videos include creative direction?

Always. You bring the finished track — I build the concept, direct the shoot, and cut the video. One vision from first frame to final export.

Got a video in your head?

Tell me about it. Quote within one business day — and your production date locks with a deposit, right on the calendar.
