How does booking work?

BOOKING · LIVE CALENDAR · CENTRAL TIME
How do I book a shoot with Taylormade Creative?

Pick your service on the booking page, choose a real open time on the live calendar — every slot you see is actually available, shown in Central time — and pay online with secure payment by Stripe. The date is yours the moment payment goes through, and a confirmation email lands in your inbox instantly.

What happens after I book?

Three emails, zero chasing. An instant confirmation when your booking goes through, a prep email a few days before the shoot so you show up ready, and a reminder the day before. You always know exactly where things stand.

What if my project is custom or isn't on the calendar?

Send a short inquiry through the booking page and I reply personally with a real quote within one business day. No form letters, no phone tag — a real number for your actual project.

Can I reschedule my shoot?

Life happens. Reach out and we'll figure it out together — reply to any email from me or send a message through the client portal, and we'll land on a date that works.

How do I contact Taylormade Creative with a question?

Already booked? Reply to any email or message me through the client portal. New here? Reach out through the booking page or on Instagram @taylormade_creative — I answer inquiries within one business day.

What does it cost?

PRICING · REAL NUMBERS
How much do digitals cost?

$100 flat. Pick a time on the live calendar, pay online, done — no quote needed.

How much does a website cost?

Web design runs in four tiers: an Ad Landing Page at $1,200, the Starter Business Website at $2,000 — the most popular pick — a Premium Business Website at $3,500, and Custom or Multi-Site builds starting at $6,000.

How is everything else priced?

Quoted per project, because scope drives cost — a music video and a headshot session aren't the same job, and neither is your project. Send an inquiry with what you're making and I reply with a real quote within one business day.

How does the deposit work?

A 50% deposit locks your date; the balance is due on shoot day. That's the standard term across the studio — simple and the same for everyone.

What happens after the shoot?

DELIVERY · PRIVATE CLIENT PORTAL
How long does it take to get my photos or video?

About two weeks is the standard turnaround, with two rounds of edits included on every project. You're never left wondering where your files are.

How do I receive my final files?

Everything delivers through your private client portal — final photos and video in one place, ready when you are. No sketchy download links, no expiring transfers.

How does photo selection work?

After a photo shoot, your proofing gallery goes live in your private client portal. You browse, pick your favorites, and the finals land right back in the same portal.

Do you travel outside Dallas–Fort Worth?

Based in Dallas–Fort Worth, shoots everywhere. Travel gets scoped into your quote up front — just tell me where the shoot is when you inquire.

Ready? The calendar is open.

Pick a real time slot, pay online, and your date is locked — confirmation in your inbox before you close the tab.
