PRIVACY

Taylormade Camera iOS app Last updated July 9, 2026

It never leaves your iPhone.

Taylormade Camera is a camera and video-editing app built by Taylormade Creative. This page explains, in plain language, exactly what the app does and doesn't do with your data. The short version: there's no server, no account, and no tracking — everything you shoot and edit stays on your device.

The short version

NO SERVER · NO ACCOUNT · NO TRACKING
Does Taylormade Camera collect or transmit any of my data?

No. The app has no server, makes no network requests, and has no analytics, advertising, or crash-reporting SDKs of any kind. There is nothing for it to send, and nowhere for it to send it. Every video, script, and preset you create is stored only in the app's private storage on your iPhone.

Do I need to create an account?

No. Taylormade Camera has no sign-in, no user accounts, and no cloud sync. There's nothing tied to your identity because the app never asks for one.

What the app asks permission for

PERMISSIONS · WHY EACH ONE EXISTS
Camera and microphone

Used only to record video and audio when you tap record. Recordings are written straight to the app's private storage on your iPhone. They are never uploaded anywhere.

Photo library

Used two ways, both of which you control directly: importing existing videos into a project when you choose them from your library, and saving your finished exports back to Photos when you tap Export. The app never reads your library in the background.

Bluetooth (external microphones)

Used only to route audio from a paired Bluetooth microphone into your recording, through Apple's standard audio session APIs. The app doesn't scan for or connect to any other kind of Bluetooth device, and doesn't use Bluetooth for tracking or location.

Face detection for Skin Tone Guard

The optional Skin Tone Guard uses Apple's on-device Vision framework to find faces in the live camera preview and keep exposure balanced on skin tones. This runs entirely on your iPhone's chip in real time — no image, face data, or measurement is ever saved, stored, or sent anywhere. Turn it off anytime from the camera screen.

Where your content lives

ON-DEVICE STORAGE · YOUR CONTROL
Where are my recordings and projects stored?

Inside the app's private storage area on your iPhone, in a space only Taylormade Camera can access. Nothing is written outside that sandbox except the final video you explicitly export to Photos.

How do I delete my data?

Delete individual clips, scripts, or edit projects right in the app, or delete the app itself — either instantly and permanently removes that content from your device. Because nothing is copied anywhere else, there's no separate copy to ask us to remove.

Does the app share data with Taylormade Creative or anyone else?

No. Taylormade Creative, the maker of this app, has no access to anything you record or edit. There is no channel for that data to reach us — the app simply doesn't have one built in.

A few more things

CHILDREN · CHANGES · CONTACT
Children's privacy

Taylormade Camera is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect information from anyone, of any age — it doesn't collect information at all, from anyone.

Will this policy change?

If it ever does, the update date at the top of this page will change and the new version will be posted here — same URL, so anything linking to this page always points at the current policy.

How do I ask a question about this?

Reach out through the contact page or message @taylormade_creative on Instagram — Taylormade Creative is a one-person DFW studio and every message gets a real, personal reply.
