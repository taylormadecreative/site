Questions, answered

ALL FAQS →
How does booking a shoot work?

Two ways. Digitals sessions book instantly: pick a real open time on the live calendar, pay online, done — confirmation, prep, and reminder emails are automatic. Everything else starts with a short inquiry; I personally reply with a quote within one business day, and a 50% deposit locks your date.

How fast do I get my photos and video back?

Standard turnaround is about two weeks, with two rounds of edits included. Everything is delivered through your private client portal — proofs, selects, finals, and video.

Do you travel outside Dallas–Fort Worth?

Based in DFW, shooting everywhere. Travel gets scoped into your quote so there are never surprise costs.

What does the deposit do?

A 50% deposit locks your date on the production calendar, with the balance due on shoot day. Digitals sessions are a flat $100 paid when you book.

Ready when you are.

Dates open on the calendar right now. Book a session in sixty seconds, or tell me about your project and I'll take it from there.
