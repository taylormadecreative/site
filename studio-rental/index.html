How does booking the studio work?

Pick hourly, half-day, or full-day, choose a real open slot on the live calendar, and pay online. You're confirmed instantly, and your confirmation email includes the exact address and access details — plus a reminder before your booking.

What does it cost?

$60/hr on weekdays, $75/hr on weekends. Half days (4 hours) are $240 weekday / $300 weekend; full days (8 hours) are $480 weekday / $600 weekend. Paid by card at booking — no deposits, no invoicing back-and-forth.

What's included — and what should I bring?

The space: natural light, hardwood floors, track lighting, and kitchen access. Pro lighting rents with the room — Nova P300 ($100), 300C ($50), Spotlight ($25), Amaran F22 ($30), and a smoke machine ($15). Tick what you need at booking and it's charged with your total. There are no cameras on site — bring your own cameras and gear.

Where is the studio?

Fort Worth, Texas. The exact address and access details go out automatically with your booking confirmation.

Can I book something recurring or longer than a day?

Yes — start a project inquiry from the booking page and tell me your schedule. Recurring classes and multi-day productions get set up personally.
