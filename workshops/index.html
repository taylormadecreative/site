ALL FAQS →
Are workshops in person or virtual?

Both. I teach in person anywhere in Dallas–Fort Worth and virtually anywhere else. The format doesn't change — everyone builds, nobody just watches.

How technical does my team need to be?

Not at all. Every session is built around your team's actual level — the scope call tells me where people are, and the curriculum meets them there. Complete beginners are welcome.

What does a workshop cost?

Every workshop is quoted per project, scoped to your group size and how deep the material goes. Send an inquiry and I reply within one business day with a real quote.

What do we need to provide?

Laptops for your people and a projector or screen for the room. Everything else — curriculum, exercises, examples, and follow-up materials — comes with me.

Can this become a recurring program?

Yes. Multi-session programs — a series that builds skills over weeks, or a standing training cadence — get scoped in your quote right alongside single sessions.

Bring a workshop to your team.

Tell me who's in the room and what you want them to walk out able to do. Custom quote within one business day.
